Educational Experience

Education History

London

I moved to London when I was eight years old and lived there for around 4 years. I went to a school called Hill House, it was where Prince Charles went! I really loved that school and everything it taught me. The picture below is of an event called field day at Hill House. It is where the whole school came together and particpated in athletic events and at the end of the day there is a ceremony where everyone flies a flag from the country they are from.

Middle School

I returned back to Connecticut for middle school and went to a school called New Canaan Country School. I met one of my favorite teachers at this school who showed me how to appreciate the beauty of writing - he encouraged me to write a lot and then it turned into a passion.

France

The summer before going into highschool I moved to France for the summer to learn language more fluently, but through the exposure to french culture. It was definitely out of my comfort zone but it ended up being a lot of fun. I don't take French anymore and I'm not sure why I stopped, I was getting quite good.

High school

I attended Wooster School for all of high school. I met my best friends for life and met some wonderful teachers who turned into role models for me.
